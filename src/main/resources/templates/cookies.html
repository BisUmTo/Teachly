1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Teachly uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure the proper functioning of our service, particularly for managing authentication via Google OAuth. We use only strictly necessary cookies to enable secure access and manage the user's session. No cookies for profiling, analytics, or advertising purposes are used.

2. Which Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Authentication and Session Management Cookies:

These cookies are used solely to securely identify the user who has logged in via Google OAuth and to maintain the user's session during site navigation. Without these cookies, the service would not function properly.

Note: These cookies do not store personal information beyond what is strictly necessary for login functionality and are considered essential for delivering the requested service.

3. Purpose of the Cookies

The cookies used by Teachly serve the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To allow users to securely access the service via Google OAuth.
  • Session Management: To keep the user's session active during navigation, ensuring continuous and secure service use.

4. Cookie Duration

The authentication cookies are session cookies and are automatically deleted when the browser is closed. In some cases, they may be set with a limited duration to ensure proper service operation (for example, to prevent unauthorized persistent sessions).

5. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of data via strictly necessary cookies is based on our legitimate interest and the need to perform a contract (i.e., the provision of authentication and session management services) in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and the applicable ePrivacy legislation.
